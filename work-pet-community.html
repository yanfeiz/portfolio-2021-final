Pet Community

Interaction Design/ App/ 2021
×
To increase the understanding of all pet owners to other pets in the community, so as to improve the possibility of finding lost pets, is my main feature focused on in this app. What's more, the united community engages unexpected power which reflects in every aspects of life. In terms of pets, people no need to worry about their pets get lost, in that when they walk in the vicinity, no matter other pet owners or property staffs notice them and pick them out, then definitely lead them to a safe place and contact their owners.
